Roadmap

A transparent view of how Bita Digital Hub grows over time — new tools, platform updates, automation features, and AI systems designed to support entrepreneurs and creators.

Q1 — Foundation & AI Integration

  • Launch About, Product, and Roadmap pages
  • Upgrade all tools to unified UI framework
  • AI-assisted content rewriting (SEO + text tools)
  • AI scoring for SEO and website analysis tools
  • Internal analytics for tool usage

Q2 — User Accounts & Saved Workflows

  • Email-based authentication (magic link login)
  • Saved tool settings and workflow continuity
  • AI “Next Step” suggestions for each tool
  • Performance dashboard for creators and small businesses
  • Private beta launch of Pro version

Q3 — Automation & Pro Features

  • AI-driven content pipelines
  • AI business advisor (content, SEO, funnels)
  • Batch-processing tools: conversions, cleanup, metadata
  • Priority processing for Pro users
  • Google Drive and Dropbox integrations

Q4 — API, Integrations & Ecosystem

  • Public API for agencies and automation developers
  • AI plugin marketplace for creators
  • Partnership integrations with online business tools
  • Team and Enterprise features
  • Full bilingual interface (Farsi and English)

Long-Term Vision

Bita Digital Hub is evolving into a fully AI-guided business system. Instead of searching for tools or guessing what to do next, the platform will analyze goals, recommend workflows, automate repetitive tasks, and help users grow with clarity and speed.
